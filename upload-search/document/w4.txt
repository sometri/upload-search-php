Overview of Cambodia
Historical Background: Cambodia has a rich and significant history, known for the Khmer Empire, which was one of the most powerful empires in Southeast Asia from the 9th to the 15th centuries.

Culture: The country boasts a diverse culture, featuring traditional arts such as dance, music, and the Khmer language.

Population: Cambodia has a population of approximately 16 million people. The primary language spoken is Khmer, used in everyday communication.

Economy: The Cambodian economy has been rapidly developing, with key sectors including tourism, agriculture, and industry.

Tourist Attractions: Siem Reap and the Angkor Wat temple complex are among the most famous tourist destinations.

Cambodia is a fascinating country for those interested in exploring its rich culture and history.
